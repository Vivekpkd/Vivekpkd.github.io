ADAS Fundamentals

Self-driving cars aren't magic. They are a fusion of sensors.

Sensor Fusion

1. Radar: Good for speed and distance (Adaptive Cruise Control). Works in fog/rain. 2. Lidar: Creates 3D point cloud maps. Expensive but accurate. 3. Cameras: Good for object classification (Stop signs, Lanes).

The Perception Loop

1. Sense: Gather raw data. 2. Perceive: Detect objects and lanes. 3. Plan: Calculate path. 4. Act: Steer and Accelerate.
